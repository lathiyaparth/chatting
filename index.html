How to Create Chat

(I) Add Member

  1. Step 1: Open the chat.
  2. Step 2: Click on the plus (+) button.
  3. Step 3: Enter the relative code.
  4. Step 4: Click "OK".
  5. Step 5: Chat starts.

(II) How to Share Your Connection Address

  1. Step 1: Open the chat.
  2. Step 2: Tap on the top-right share icon.
  3. Step 3: Select media and share.

(This chat is text-based only. The app provides privacy, so manual member addition is required. In the next update, automatic member addition and group chat initiation will be implemented.)
